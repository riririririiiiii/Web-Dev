Recipe #3: Pan-Seared Steak

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Ribeye Steaks 2 lb
Garlic 2 cloves
rosemary 1 sprig
unsalted butter 2 tbsp

Step 1: Season the steaks and sear them in a hot pan.

Step 2: Add butter, garlic, and rosemary for flavor.

Step 3: Let the steak rest before slicing and serving.
